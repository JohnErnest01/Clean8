About CLEAN8

Student-led innovation bringing comfort, cleanliness, and care into every home.

Who We Are

Clean8 is a student-led home cleaning service created by the University Team of Innovators (UTI). We aim to provide practical and meaningful solutions that make everyday life easier.

Our Team Mission

The University Team of Innovators exists to serve the people and create meaningful change through innovation. We develop practical solutions that improve everyday living.

Our Mission with Clean8

Clean8 was created to offer a fast, reliable, and convenient way for people to keep their homes clean. We know life gets busy—so we take care of the cleaning, giving you more time for what matters.

Why Choose Clean8?

  • Fast Response Time – We value your time and ensure prompt service.
  • Reliable Cleaners – Our team delivers consistent, high-quality results.
  • Student-Led Innovation – Fresh ideas and creative solutions at the core.
  • Flexible Plans – One-time cleaning or weekly subscriptions — your choice.

At Clean8, we believe a clean home is more than just neatness — it’s peace of mind, comfort, and extra time for the things you love.
